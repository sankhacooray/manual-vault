Manual Vault

Trash the paper. Scan the QR code, keep the PDF in your own Google Drive.

Open Manual Vault

Sign in with any Google account — your manuals are stored in a "Manual Vault" folder in your Drive. The app can only touch files it created.

Scan & Save

Point your camera at the QR code printed on the manual. The PDF opens instantly and is saved to Drive in the background.

Claude indexing

Bring your own Anthropic API key and each manual is read, titled, categorized and tagged automatically.

Search everything

Find the right manual by brand, model, feature or problem — the search runs over the Claude-built index.

Scoped-down access

Uses the narrow drive.file permission: the app cannot read, modify or delete anything else in your Google Drive.
